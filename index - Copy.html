Our Food Menu

  • Jollof Rice

    Jollof rice is a beloved dish that holds a special place in the hearts and plates of many West Africans. This vibrant and flavorful one-pot dish is a staple at celebrations, gatherings, and everyday meals across the region.

  • Fufu

    Fufu is a traditional dish that plays a fundamental role in the culinary landscape of West Africa, renowned for its unique texture and versatility. It serves as a staple accompaniment to various soups, stews, and sauces, making it a beloved component of daily meals.

  • Plain Rice & Stew

    This dish often serves as a staple in various cuisines, providing a comforting and satisfying option for both everyday meals and special occasions.

  • Waakye

  • Banku & Talipa
